# Weighted Grades

Not all grades are equal!
- Homework: Worth 50% (0.5)
- Test: Worth 50% (0.5)

If you get 100 on Homework and 50 on Test:
`(100 * 0.5) + (50 * 0.5)`
`50 + 25 = 75`

Your grade is 75!

**Why:** Tests are usually harder and worth more!
