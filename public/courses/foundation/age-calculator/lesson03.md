## Your Mission
How old will you be in the year 2050? Let's find out!

## The Math
```
Your age in 2050 = Current age + Years until 2050
Years until 2050 = 2050 - 2025 = 25 years
```

So if you're 10 now: 10 + 25 = 35 years old in 2050!
