# CHALLENGE 3: Fight to Death

Loop until one dies!
`while f1.hp > 0 and f2.hp > 0:`

### Your Task:
1. Two fighters (High HP).
2. While both are alive: F1 attacks F2, then F2 attacks F1.
3. Print "Round Over".
4. When loop ends, print "Game Over".

### Practical Use
This loop IS the game. It runs thousands of times per second in modern engines.
